Behind The Glam

Welcome to OpalGlam

Founded with a vision to redefine beauty with elegance and individuality, Opal Glam was born out of a passion for artistry, inclusivity, and self-expression. Inspired by the iridescent beauty of opal stones, our brand celebrates every shade, undertone, and personality.

From humble beginnings in a home studio to becoming a growing favorite among beauty enthusiasts, Opal Glam is more than a makeup brand—it’s a movement. Every product is thoughtfully crafted to empower, enhance confidence, and add a touch of glam to every moment

We believe that beauty isn’t one-size-fits-all. Whether soft and subtle or bold and fearless, Opal Glam helps you express your unique glow—your way.
